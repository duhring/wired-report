Epstein Island's Secret Data

WIRED Investigation Reveals Surveillance of 200+ Mobile Phones

Original WIRED Investigation • 8:37 duration

Key Investigation Highlights

This investigation uncovered location data from mobile phones belonging to visitors of Jeffrey Epstein's private island between 2016-2019. The data reveals precise tracking of wealthy individuals, their movements, and connections - offering unprecedented insight into the surveillance industry and high-profile networks.
